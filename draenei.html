Draenei

Overview:

The Draenei are followers of the Naaru and worshipers of the Holy Light.
They originally hail from the distant world of Argus, fleeing after Sargeras tried to corrupt them.
They then settled on the Orcish homeworld of Draenor, where after a period of peace, they were brutally murdered during Guldan's corruption of the Orcs.
Finally they settled in Azeroth, to seek aid in their battle against the Burning Legion. Draenei were introduced in the Burning Crusade expansion.
